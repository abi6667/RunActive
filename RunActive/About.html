About RunActive

RunActive is a commuity created for people who love running and fitness. Our goal is to inspire runners to stay active and participtae in marathon events. The term "run active" likely refers to the growing trend of running as a community activity and fitness practice. In India, running culture is booming, with over 1,500 annual events generating $450 million in revenue and attracting 2.5 million registered runners. Run clubs are emerging, providing a sense of community and motivation for individuals to engage in running. Run activation is a concept that involves warm-up exercises to prepare the body for running, improving performance and reducing injury risk.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to inspire people to stay active through running. Global Running Foundation: Focuses on health and opportunity, aiming to raise awareness and inspire action around causes that matter most. Every Person Run Club: Empowers individuals of all abilities to experience the joy of running in a welcoming and supportive community. Striders Miles Pvt Ltd: Empowers individuals and communities through coaching, wellness initiatives, and professionally managed running events.

Our vision

Our vision is to create a world where running is accessible and enjoyable for everyone. We strive to build a community that celebrates diversity, encourages growth, and supports each other both on and off the pavement. Through our collective passion for running, we aim to break down barriers, promote healthy living, and contribute to a more connected and empathetic society. Inclusivity: We welcome runners of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds, ensuring that everyone feels valued and respected. No one is left behind, and all paces are celebrated. Support: We are committed to supporting each other in our running journeys, offering encouragement, guidance, and friendship every step of the way. Community: Our club is more than just a place to run; it’s a TEAM. We build meaningful connections and foster a sense of belonging, both within our club and in the wider community.

Running is a powerful exercise that enhances physical health, mental well-being, longevity, and overall quality of life. Physical Health Benefits Running strengthens the cardiovascular system, improving heart health and lowering the risk of heart disease. Studies show that runners are about 50% less likely to die from heart disease compared to non-runners, and regular running lowers resting heart rate, indicating more efficient heart function. It also helps maintain healthy body weight, burns calories effectively, and strengthens muscles and bones, reducing the risk of osteoporosis and muscle wasting. Additionally, running improves lung function, boosts immunity, and enhances overall physical fitness. WebMD +1 Mental and Cognitive Benefits Running has significant mental health benefits. It triggers the release of endorphins, which improve mood, reduce stress, and combat depression. Regular running enhances cognitive function, memory, and focus, and may reduce the risk of cognitive decline and Alzheimer’s disease. It also promotes better sleep quality, helping the body repair and rejuvenate. New Health Advisor +2 Longevity and Quality of Life Research indicates that running can increase lifespan by 3 years on average and reduce all-cause mortality by 25-30%. Beyond longevity, running improves life quality by maintaining physical abilities, supporting healthy body composition, and enhancing hormone regulation. Even moderate running, such as a 10-minute daily jog, provides measurable health benefits. Marathon Handbook +3 Social and Lifestyle Benefits Running can foster social connections through group runs, clubs, or community events. It encourages outdoor activity, which can improve mood and provide a sense of accomplishment. Running also promotes discipline, goal-setting, and resilience, which can positively influence other areas of life. Marathon Handbook Summary In essence, running is more than just a form of exercise. It supports heart and lung health, strengthens muscles and bones, enhances mental well-being, improves sleep, aids weight management, and increases longevity. Its benefits extend to cognitive function, emotional health, and social engagement, making it a holistic activity for overall wellness.
